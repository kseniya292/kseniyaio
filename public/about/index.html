About

Co-founder and smart contract developer at Upstate Interactive, a software consultancy headquartered in Syracuse, NY.

I wrote my first line of HTML & CSS in 2015. Within the next two years I quit my job, started a company, packed my life in my car and moved across the country.

I’m passionate about blockchain technology and getting more women into tech. In Syracuse, I co-founded Women in Coding to provide education resources for women in the community. In 2018, I founded a group called DAppLadies to teach female developers how to build decentralized applications. I’m also a co-founder of The Rabbit Hole, a woman-powered blockchain community that promotes the participation and leadership of women in blockchain.
